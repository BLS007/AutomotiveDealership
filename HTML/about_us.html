Why Buy From Extreme Motors?

Welcome to Extreme Motors! We are Louisiana's premier new and used car dealership, ready and willing to help you find what your looking for! Extreme Motors was founded by Jacques Rioux, Marcellus Rioux's father, in 1978. Since then, we have grown from selling new and used brands along the lines of GMC, Honda and Toyota to selling more luxurious brands. Brands such as BMW, Ferrari, Lamborghini and Mercedes-Benz were always Jacques vision for this dealership. Jacques vision eventually became a reality and has been retired from the dealership since 2010. When Jacques retired from the dealership in 2010, he passed the torch on to his son Marcellus.

Marcellus worked with his father at the dealership since he was a child, Marcellus's first real job at Extreme Motors was in the 6th grade. He worked as a car detailer and over time worked his way up the ranks and learned everything about the company. Marcellus and his team continue to find better ways to improve the overall customer service experience here at Extreme Motors and their work has shown making Extreme Motors rated as one of the best dealerships to work for in 2021.
